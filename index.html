About me

Asim is a computer scientist, Assistant Lecturer and programmer who is passionate about learning new things, solving challenging problems in the field of Project Management.

Asim is a software developer with over five years’ experience designing, implementing, and working closely with other full-stack developers, UX designers, business analysts and project managers. Because of his passion in writing codes and learning new technologies quickly, he obtained his Bachelor’s Degree and Master’s Degree in Computer Science from Nile University of Nigeria, where he graduated as one of the best students in his department and was awarded the VC award of overall best behaved graduating male student.

Asim’s outstanding final year project secured him a job offers immediately after he graduated.

Asim is the founder of Nightowl Technology, an IT investment firm that gives birth to two of his Startups Suya Craze Nigeria and Mallmark the Mall of the Internet. Mallmark is an online platform for anyone to sell and buy African native products. Prior founding Nightowl, Asim got his entrepreneurial spirit after initiating a trip as the Vice President of a student club for the first time in the school which generated more than 2-millionnaira income to the club from zero savings within 2months. The trip has now become an annual tradition for the club.
